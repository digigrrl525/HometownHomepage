Visit Saint Louis

Enjoy learning and cultural opportunities in this "Gateway to the West".

Top 3 activities to do in Saint Louis

Gateway Arch

At 630 feet (190 m), the Gateway Arch is a great way to test your height tolerance.

Forest Park

Forest Park is almost twice as large as Central Park in New York City and host to the Muny Theatre, Art Museum, Zoo, and more.

Science Center

Founded in 1963, it includes a science museum and a planetarium, and is situated in Forest Park. Admission is free.
